30$ / Story Point Business Model

Story points are a common measure in software development to estimate the effort needed to complete a task. They encompass the complexity, risk, and repetition involved, providing a more accurate reflection of work than merely counting hours.

For businesses

Estimate the story points for each ticket based on the task's complexity and effort required. Payment is only processed once the Pull Request is merged, ensuring satisfaction with the completed task.

For developers

Pick up a ticket, see the story points estimated by the company, work on the task, and get paid promptly once your Pull Request is merged and approved.
